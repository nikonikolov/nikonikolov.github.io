Scholarships

Information for Applicants:

Below are listed some of the scholarships that high-school students can apply for. We encourage you to have a look at all of the provided opportunities as they might become available to you at some point during your high-school education. Please keep in mind that for most of the provided scholarships you should prepare your applications carefully and remember that nobody will award money to an applicant who wrote his application overnight. Applications which do not require essays or some kind of writing are usually strongly based on the applicants' previous accomplishments. Note this list is not considered conclusive and applicants are encouraged to look for further opportunities.


Information for Organizations:

If you or organization awards some kind of scholarship for high-school students and you would like to see it listed on our website, please contact us using the links on the bottom.
