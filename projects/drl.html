Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robot Picking

Overview

I built this project during a 10-week internship at Ocado Technology in summer 2017. I implemented an algorithm for a robotic arm to pick objects from a basket based on uncalibrated camera input. The approach was primarily based on the paper [1] Sergey Levine, Peter Pastor, Alex Krizhevsky, and Deirdre Quillen. Learning hand-eye coordination for robotic grasping with deep learning and large-scale data collection. CoRR, abs/1603.02199, 2016. The implementation I wrote is in TensorFlow. For picking, I used a suction cup system developed by Ocado Technology. I deployed the algorithm on a UR10 robotic arm.
