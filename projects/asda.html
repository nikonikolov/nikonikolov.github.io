Autonomous Snack Delivery Android (ASDA)

Overview

We built this project in a group of 8 as part of our Third Year Group Project module at Imperial. I was the technical lead of our team and our supervisor was Dr Petar Kormushev from the Robot Intelligence Lab at Imperial. We used his robot DE NIRO, which is a modified version of Baxter placed on a wheelchair.

For our project we enabled DE NIRO to autonomously navigate in the EEE department building at Imperial, buy snacks from the cafe and deliver them to people.

Implementation

We based our implementation on ROS. We used extensively packages such as octomap, ROS navigation, hector_mapping and PCL. We also used visual servoing to make the robot press the lift buttons on its own, rather than integrating with the lift control software.
