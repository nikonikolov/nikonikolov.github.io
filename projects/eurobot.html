Eurobot

Overview

Eurobot is an international amateur robotics contest open to teams of students. In 2017, I was part of the Imperial College team. The goal was to make the robot build a lunar base fully autonomously in 90 seconds.

I worked on designing all of the robot subsystems and oversaw the whole development of the robot. I also actively contributed to the localization algorithm.
