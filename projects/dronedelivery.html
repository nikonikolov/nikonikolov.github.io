Drone Delivery

Overview

The goal of this project is to build a drone that is able to deliver food around Brown University, Providence, RI. I am working together with Aaron Zhang - a student at Brown. We are developing a special mechanism that allows for faster and more secure deliveries. We are using a DJI M-100 as a development platform, but our mechanism can be deployed on any other drone. The project is named A2Z drone delivery.

With our mechanism, the package is lowered on a tether and the drone does not need to land. The package can fall down freely (similar to a fishing rod) and stop right before it hits the ground. This has numerous advantages, such as substantially decreased delivery time, stronger drone stability, safety (unfriendly people cannot hold the tether and prevent the drone from flying off as the tether can be released by the drone).
