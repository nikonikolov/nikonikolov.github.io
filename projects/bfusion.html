Bayesian Fusion for SLAM

Overview

This is a research project I have been working on together with Stefan Leutenegger at the Dyson Robotics Lab at Imperial. This work is also still unpublished, so I cannot discuss it in detail. However, it is to come out very soon, so stay tuned.

My involvement included investigating and developing a state-of-the-art probabilistic formulation for fusing depth camera measurements and using them for SLAM and 3D reconstruction. I also helped for the development of a SLAM framework and implemented our model in it.
