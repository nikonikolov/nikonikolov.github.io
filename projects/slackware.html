Slackware Linux Package Development

Overview

I have been contributing to Slackware Linux since 2016. I am often new providing SlackBuild scripts for robotics and ML packages in order to make them readily available on Slackware. I have also contributed to ROS in order to make it compatible with Slackware. Here is a list of all packages I have enabled for Slackware:

  • ROS Kinetic
  • libdart
  • console_bridge
  • cudnn
  • flann
  • gazebo
  • ignition-math2
  • ignition-transport
  • nlopt
  • pcl
  • sdformat
  • urdfdom
  • urdfdom_headers
  • xf86-input-libinput
