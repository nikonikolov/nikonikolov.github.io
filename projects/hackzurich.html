Fridge Content Tracker

Overview

We built this project during HackZurich in September 2016. My team and I wanted to provide real-time fridge contents information. Our approach was to process pictures of grocery receipts to track what goes in the fridge. The biggest chellenges were to process tilted or upside-down images of receipts and to analyze the information on the receipt since there is no unified format that different stores use. We built an algorithm which used a combination of Fourier Transform phase analysis, Google OCR API and language parsing. We also built a mobile app for the end user.
