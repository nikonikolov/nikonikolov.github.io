About

I am Nikolay Nikolov, an undergratuate student at Imperial College London currently pursuing MEng in Electronic and Information Engineering. In September 2017 I will be starting my exchange at ETH Zurich where I will be doing the 4th year of my studies, including my master thesis.

My main interest is exploring Deep Reinforcement Learning with applications to Robotics. I am also excited about areas such as Deep Learning, SLAM and Computer Vision.

For the summer of 2017, I am interning at Ocado Technology as Robotics Research Intern. Previously I was part of Stefan Leutenegger's research group at the Dyson Robotics Lab at Imperial. I also worked together with Petar Kormushev from the Robot Intelligence Lab and did research with Mirko Kovac at the Aerial Robotics Lab.
