About

I am Nikolay Nikolov, an undergratuate student at Imperial College London pursuing MEng in Electronic and Information Engineering. Currently, I am doing the 4th year of my studies, including my Master's Thesis, as exchange student at ETH Zurich.

My main interest is in the area of Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robotics. I am also excited about fields such as Deep Learning, SLAM and Computer Vision.

Currently, I am part of Prof. Andreas Krause's research group at the Learning & Adaptive Systems Lab at ETH. Previously, I was part of Prof. Stefan Leutenegger's group at the Dyson Robotics Lab at Imperial. I have also worked together with Prof. Petar Kormushev from the Robot Intelligence Lab and Prof. Mirko Kovac at the Aerial Robotics Lab.
