Drone Delivery

Overview

The goal of this project is to build a drone that is able to deliver food around Brown University in Providence, RI. I am working together with Aaron Zhang - a student at Brown. We are developing a special mechanism that allows for faster and more secure deliveries. We are using a DJI M-100 as a development platform, but out mechanism can be deployed on any other drone. The project is named A2Z drone delivery.

The advantage of our mechanism is that the package is lowered on a tether and thus the drone does not to land. Another great feature of our design is that the drone cannot be stolen by unfriendly people: if someone starts pulling the tether, it can be released by the drone. This way the drone can continue flying undisturbed.
