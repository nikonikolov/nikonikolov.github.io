Eurobot

Overview

Eurobot is an international amateur robotics contest open to teams of students. This year Imperial College is sending a team to compete for it and I am the happy person who is the technical leader of our team. The competition is structured in three independent challenges that our robot needs to complete in 90 seconds. This year the topic is "Moon Village" and our team is focusing on the task of building a lunar base and launching a rocket from our robot. The robots must be fully autonomous.
